❓ Help

How to use:

1. Prepare your Excel file with product data
2. Click the upload area and select your file
3. Click "Upload Products" button
4. Wait for the upload to complete
5. Check the status message for results

What happens:

• New products are added to the database
• Existing products (same code) are updated
• Products become immediately available on the app
